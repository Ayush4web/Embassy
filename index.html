We Make An Offer You Can't Refuse

MEET

This is a session, where I sit together and break some ice! Gradually, the aim is to get to understand the DNA of my client business, X-Y-Z plans, so that going forward, I can be on the same page with them!

STRATEGIZE

This is the most crucial stage, where I put in the place the rudiments of a plan that can transform the clients product/business into an unforgettable brand. I call it the 'Mission Possible' stage.

IDENTITY

This takes the relationship a bit deeper, where I identify their needs, spell out their project plans, create milestones, set deadlines; agree on deliverables, and so on. It's the 'dream stage.'

DETAILS

This is the cherry-picking stage, where the best ideas are selected and fine tuned to match the precise requirements. I call it the 'Gestation' stage.

IDEATE

Things begin to move very fast at this stage. There is an adrenaline rush and ideas begin to fly thick and fast from all quarters. I identify it as the 'Conception' stage.

EXECUTE

After all the sweat and toil, this is the 'Birth' stage, that comes with all the delivery pangs, after which I finally hand the client their dream - fresh from the stock, and just as they had thought of! Congratulations!
